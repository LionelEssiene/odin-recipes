Lasagna

Description

Lasagna dish is a famous cameroonian dish originating from the north west. Originally a know for its beatiful presentation, it has as well captured the minds and won the hearts of many because of its taste. It an undescribable proper mix between sour and sweet.

It is usually served alongside Fufu. Both are a combination of the heart and soul of the cameroonian love transfered through their beautiful and talented women. it is usally said, once you eat cameroonian lasagna, you fall in love with cameroon. And be careful not to fall in love with the beautiful lady serving this delicious meal you might not want to leave cameroon anymore.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Greens
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil 300l of water
  2. Cut tomatoes and onions into smaller pieces
  3. Cut the Greens
  4. Make slices of the sweet potatoes
  5. Pour 3 teaspoons of oil into the pan
  6. Add the water
  7. Fry tomatoes and onions
