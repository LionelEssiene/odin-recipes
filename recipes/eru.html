Eru

Description

Eru is a famous cameroonian dish originating from the north west. Originally a know for its beatiful presentation, it has as well captured the minds and won the hearts of many because of its taste. It an undescribable proper mix between sour and sweet.

It is usually served alongside Fufu. Both are a combination of the heart and soul of the cameroonian love transfered through their beautiful and talented women. it is usally said, once you eat cameroonian lasagna, you fall in love with cameroon. And be careful not to fall in love with the beautiful lady serving this delicious meal you might not want to leave cameroon anymore.

Ingredients

  • Waterleaf
  • Water Fufu
  • Kanda meat
  • Towel meat
  • Beaf meat
  • okok
  • Red oil

Steps

  1. Boil 300l of water
  2. Prepare water fufu
  3. Put water leaf in water
  4. Add okok to it
  5. Cut the meat into slices
  6. Add the meat to the eru
  7. Serve hot
